FAQ

What are the different types of bamboo?

There are over 1,400 species of bamboo, and they can be divided into two main types: clumping and running. Clumping bamboos grow in a tight clump and do not spread, while running bamboos can spread quickly and can be invasive.

How much sunlight does bamboo need?

Bamboo needs full sun to thrive. It will tolerate partial shade, but it will not grow as well.

How much water does bamboo need?

Bamboo needs regular watering, especially during the first year after planting. Once established, bamboo can tolerate some drought, but it will grow better with regular watering.

What is the best soil for bamboo?

Bamboo grows best in well-drained soil. It can tolerate a wide range of soil types, but it will not grow well in clay or sandy soil.

How do I care for bamboo?

Bamboo is a relatively low-maintenance plant. It needs to be watered regularly, especially during the first year after planting. It should also be fertilized once a year in the spring.
